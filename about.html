My Story

From the clinic
to your door

Hi, I'm Lisa. I've spent my career as a Licensed Practical Nurse, and along the way I discovered a deep passion for foot health — one of the most overlooked parts of a person's overall wellbeing.

I completed my Advanced Foot Care Nursing certification and founded The Tootsy Nurse to bring something Kelowna was missing: foot care that is both clinically safe and genuinely luxurious.

Whether you're a diabetic client who needs careful monitoring, a senior who wants to feel comfortable, or simply someone who deserves a little indulgence — I am here for you.
