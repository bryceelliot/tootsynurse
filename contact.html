Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you travel to my home?

Yes — that's the whole idea. Lisa brings everything needed right to your door: a portable basin, sterilized tools, premium products, and her nursing expertise. No driving, no waiting rooms.

Is it safe if I have diabetes?

Absolutely. Lisa is specifically trained in diabetic foot care. She performs a full skin and circulation assessment, trims nails safely to prevent ingrown nails, and manages calluses with nurse-grade care. Many of her regular clients are diabetic.

How do I pay?

Cash or e-transfer are accepted. Payment is made at the time of your appointment.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Life happens — just give Lisa a call or text at least 24 hours in advance and she'll be happy to reschedule. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Do you serve care homes and assisted living?

Yes. Lisa regularly visits care facilities throughout the Kelowna area. Group bookings for facilities are welcome — contact Lisa directly to arrange.

How far in advance should I book?

Calling a week or two ahead is usually enough.

How much does it cost?

Mobile Foot Care is $65 and a Home Spa Pedicure is $85. Senior & Diabetic Care is available by request. A travel fee may apply outside the local area.

What areas do you serve?

The Tootsy Nurse serves Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Peachland and surrounding areas. Lisa comes to you — your home, care facility, or office.

"Treat your feet."
